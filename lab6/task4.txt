Class:
A blueprint for the object comprising of data members and methods.

Object:
 A real world entity, having attributes and behaviour as its definition. Can be said as the implementation of the class.

Attribute:
 Attribute is the class's data needed and used with its methods. Attributes are also termed as "instance variables" or "data members".

Behavior: 
Behaviour is the functionality needed to be in the Object to carry out certain tasks individually or collectively. Behaviour is also termed as "Methods" in OOP.
